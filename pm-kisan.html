PM Kisan Registration, Status & Scheme Information

PM Kisan Yojana Kya Hai?

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana ek kendriya yojana hai jiske tahat patra kisanon ko arthik sahayata di jati hai.

Yojana Ke Fayde

  • Kisanon ko arthik sahayata
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
  • Bank Account mein seedha bhugtan

Registration Process

  1. Official PM Kisan portal par jaayein.
  2. Registration option chunen.
  3. Aavashyak jankari bharein.
  4. Documents submit karein.
  5. Application submit karein.

Status Check

Registration ke baad beneficiary status aur payment details adhikarik portal par check ki ja sakti hain.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Mobile Number
  • Land Related Information (agar lagu ho)

Important Note

Yojana ke niyam, patrata aur prakriya samay ke saath badal sakte hain. Latest jankari ke liye adhikarik source check karein.
