Passport Banwane Ki Puri Jankari

Passport Kya Hai?

Passport ek official travel document hai jo kisi vyakti ki pahchan aur rashtriyata ko darshata hai.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card (agar uplabdh ho)
  • Address Proof
  • Passport Size Photo

Apply Karne Ka Process

  1. Passport Seva Portal par registration karein.
  2. Online application form bharein.
  3. Fees jama karein.
  4. Appointment book karein.
  5. Passport Seva Kendra mein verification karayein.

Important Note

Passport ki fees, documents aur process samay ke saath badal sakte hain. Apply karne se pehle adhikarik portal par latest jankari jarur check karein.
