Ayushman Card Kya Hai?

Ayushman Bharat Yojana ke tahat patr parivaron ko swasthya sambandhi suvidhaon ka labh diya jata hai.

Ayushman Card Ke Fayde

  • Health Coverage Benefits
  • Cashless Treatment Facility
  • Empanelled Hospitals Mein Upchar
  • Parivar Ke Liye Swasthya Suraksha

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Mobile Number
  • Family Details
  • Identity Documents

Ayushman Card Kaise Banwaye?

  1. Eligibility Check Karein
  2. Required Documents Ready Rakhein
  3. Official Process Follow Karein
  4. Verification Complete Karein
  5. Card Generate Hone Ke Baad Download Karein

Eligibility Check

Ayushman Card ke liye patrata sambandhit jankari official portal par check ki ja sakti hai.

Important Note

Yojana ke niyam, patrata aur prakriya samay ke saath badal sakte hain. Latest jankari ke liye official source jarur check karein.
